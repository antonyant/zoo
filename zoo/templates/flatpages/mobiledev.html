Mobile Workstation

 

The ZeroOneOne mobile workstation is used for remote development, on-site meetings and generally having a good time.

 

It is really quite an awesome bit of gear.

 

Built on an Isuzu NPS 4x4 6.5 ton chassis its really strong and can get in to almost anywhere. The base truck has had a single wheel conversion and a suspension upgrade to improve the ride and performance off road.

 

In order to keep the truck agile enough to get down winding 4x4 tracks, park in a parking space at the supermarket and go out for coffee. The living area is a bit of a compromise. That said there is a surprising amount of creature comforts packed into the box.

 

There is a lounge area for on site meetings. Desk and office chair for slogging code.

 

**pic of lounge/office setup**

 

With a 540 watt solar power system there is Plenty of power for 24/7 website development.

 

**pic of solar/power cabinet**

 

Stainless steel kitchen for when you get hungry and or need a hot cuppa. Boasting a 130lt frige/freezer and a Oven/griller/3 burner stove.

 

**pic of kitchen**

 

When you get sleepy hit the switch on the wall and lower the queen size bed down into the lounge.

 

**pic of with bed down**

 

For cleaning away the grime just have a hot shower! There is a 30lt hot water system and 300lt fresh water. For quick getaways (or to go get bread and milk) there is a crawl through to the truck cab space.
